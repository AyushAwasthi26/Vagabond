What is Vagabond?

Vagabond is more than just a travel site; it's a recommendation system designed to find your perfect journey. It takes your personal preferences—your desired travel style, budget, and activities—and processes them to find the ideal destinations and itineraries for you. Our goal is to take the stress out of planning and give you top-tier recommendations that are perfectly suited to your tastes.
