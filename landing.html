Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Vagabond, and how does it work?

+
Vagabond is your personal travel compass, not just a booking site. We use cutting-edge data analysis to understand your unique travel style, interests, and preferences. By processing this information, we provide personalized recommendations and craft the perfect journey for you. Instead of endless searching, we suggest the best destination and itinerary for your next adventure—at this very moment.

Is Vagabond free to use?

+
The core recommendation service—our personalized itinerary and destination suggestions—is completely free to use. You only pay when you decide to book a trip or package directly through our partners. This ensures you can explore your options and find the perfect trip without any upfront commitment.

How does the recommendation system work its magic?

+
Our "magic" is a powerful blend of data and intelligence. When you provide us with your travel interests, budget, and desired trip length, our proprietary algorithm processes thousands of data points on destinations, flights, hotels, and activities. We cross-reference this with real-time availability and trending traveler preferences to generate a recommendation that is both unique to you and logistically perfect.

What kind of trips can Vagabond suggest?

+
Vagabond can recommend a wide range of travel experiences. Whether you're looking for a relaxing beach getaway, a thrilling mountain adventure, a deep dive into historical cities, or a unique culinary tour, our system is designed to find a match. From solo backpacking trips to luxury family vacations, we cater to every type of traveler.
